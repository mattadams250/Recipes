Perfect Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Description

This is the best chocolate chip cookie recipe that you'll ever use. They are so perfect and soft, with amazing taste.

Ingredients

  • 190g (14 tbsp) unsalted butter, at room temp
  • 130g (2/3 cup) granulated sugar
  • 130g (2/3 cup) packed light brown sugar
  • 80g (2 large) eggs
  • 330g (2 2/3 cups) AP flour
  • 4g (1 tsp) baking soda
  • 6g (1 tsp) salt
  • 100g (2/3 cup) milk chocolate chunks
  • 100g (2/3 cup) dark chocolate chunks
  • 1.5 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 drizzle of honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Directions

  1. Leave the butter out for 2 hours to soften, or just cut into 1/8ths and microwave for 30 seconds to soften
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the 14 tbsp butter, 2/3 cup brown sugar, and 2/3 cup cane sugar using a spatula. (just mix and make sure everything gets incorporated, the mixture will be light and creamy)
  3. 3. Add in a drizzle of honey and 1 tsp of vanilla extract and mix
  4. Mix in 2 large eggs, one at a time
  5. In a separate medium-large bowl: mix together the 2 2/3 cup AP flour, 1 tsp baking soda, 1.5 tsp cornstarch, and 1 tsp of salt using a whisk, fork, or whatever you have
  6. Add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients, half at a time, and mix
  7. Mix in the chocolate chips, half at a time so they will be evenly incorporated (measure with your heart)
  8. Roll into balls, place on 2 plates with each ball close together, and place into the freezer.
  9. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  10. Just before the oven is preheated, place the cookie dough balls onto a baking sheet with a silicon mat(if you don't have one that's fine too)
  11. Bake for 10-12 minutes, take out when the edges begin to brown
  12. Let set for about 5 minutes (if you can) so the cookie will solidify. Or just eat instantly, that's what I do
