S'mores Cookies Recipe

Description

These s'mores cookies are a bit messy, but they are one of the tastiest things you'll ever make. They are so easy and tasty.

Ingredients

  • 10 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt (none if using salted butter)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 chocolate bar
  • 5 graham crackers sheets (or as many as you want)
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (or as many as you want)
  • 1 cup mini-marshmallows (or as many as you want)

Directions

  1. Melt the butter in the microwave in 30 second bursts
  2. Add both sugars cream together for 1-2 minutes. The mixture will be lighter and creamier
  3. Mix together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl (or just on top of the wet ingredients for an easier cleanup)
  4. Add in all of the toppings to your heartâ€™s content and mix
  5. Form in to dough balls of any size, the bigger the better
  6. Place on a plate in the fridge for at least an hour, up to 3 days, but ideally overnight (or in the freezer for 30 minutes)
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 7-9 minutes
  8. Take the cookies out and add a piece of chocolate on the top of each cookie
  9. Take out just as the edges are starting to get golden.
  10. Grub hard
